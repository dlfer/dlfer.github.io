Jalapeno Poppers (peperoni verdi fritti ripieni di formaggio)

    (Veg):「
  • peperoni
  • fritto
    • 」

Ingredienti

12 fresh jalapeños;

remove seeds and membrane (after 5 min microwave 600w).

cut side, fill with:

  • 1/2 formaggo latteria (type) + 1/2 philadelphia, mixed and mashed.
  • [+ pepper , +salt, +hot sauce, +egg]
  • breadcrumbs (pangrattato)
  • [+dried oregano]
  • [+salt]
  • [+pepper]

Prep

  • fry: 5-6 min 163 °C. 3 min 187 °C (in batches of 3)
yield 6
date 2020-02-18
